*Warrior Class implementation and testing complete!*

*Passive Abilities:*
_*Vanguard*_
• *Effect:* +2 Hearts (24 Max HP).
• *Cooldown:* None (Always Active).

_*Adrenaline Rush*_
• *Effect:* Grants Speed II for 5s when taking damage at low health (≤2.5 hearts).
• *Cooldown:* 3 seconds.

_*Bloodthirsty*_
• *Effect:* Heals 1.5 Hearts immediately upon killing a player.
• *Cooldown:* None.

*Active Abilities:*
_*Thou Shalt Bleed*_
• *Trigger:* Block with Shield while holding a Sword.
• *Effect:* Grants +1.5 Attack Damage (Sharpness II equivalent) to your sword for that engagement.
• *Cooldown:* None (State-based; removes when you switch items).

_*War Cry*_
• *Trigger:* Use a War Horn or Goat Horn (Right-click).
• *Effect:* Grants Strength I (+3.0 damage) for 8s to all teammates within 20 blocks.
• *Cooldown:* 120 seconds (Global).

*Custom Recipe:*
_*War Horn:* Shears (returned) + Pointed Dripstone_  

*Non-role Nerf:*
_*Fragile Shields:* Non-Warriors will receive 2x shield durability damage!_

==*Comparison*==
• *Diamond Sword*: 7 Attack damage
• Diamond Sword *+ Thou Shalt Bleed*: 8.5 Attack damage
• Diamond Sword + Thou Shalt Bleed *+ War Cry*: 11.5 Attack damage
• Overall Effect: *60% Damage bonus*

_Fighting with a Wooden Sword with both abilities on is stronger than fighting with a Netherite Sword!_

*Farmer Class*

*Passive Abilities:*
_*Photosynthesis*_
• *Effect:* Standing in direct sunlight grants Absorption I (2 golden hearts) for 30 seconds.
• *Cooldown:* Triggered after 10s in sunlight.

_*Breeding Mastery*_
• *Effect:* Reduces animal breeding cooldown to 2.5 minutes (normally 5).
• *Cooldown:* None.

_*Automated Composter*_
• *Effect:* Composters you place boost crop growth in a 9x9 area to 2x!
• *Cooldown:* None.

_*Optimized Yield*_
• *Effect:* Gain 50% additional crop yields when harvesting

*Active Abilities:*
_*Nature's Vengeance*_
• *Trigger:* Eat a super Beetroot.
• *Effect:* Summons a line of 4 Evoker Fangs in front of you to ward off enemies.
• *Cooldown:* 5 seconds.


*Non-role Nerf:*
_*Natural Incompetence:* Non-Farmers have a reduced yield when harvesting certain crops!_


==*Comparison*==
• *Composter Automation* _200%_ + *Optimized Yield* _150%_ + *Natural Incompetence* _75% enemy nerf_ = *4x enemies' farming output!*

*Enchanter Class implementation and testing complete!*

*Passive Abilities:*
_*Knowledgeable*_
• *Effect:* Refunds 1 XP level immediately after enchanting an item.
• *Cooldown:* None.

_*Alchemist*_
• *Effect:* Charge a Brewing Stand while holding ingredients to refuel it (+10 fuel).
• *Trigger:* Jumping while looking at a brewing stand holding Gold Nugget (Mainhand) and Lapis Lazuli (Offhand)
• *Cooldown:* None.

*Active Abilities:*
_*Sky Mage*_
• *Trigger:* Use a Sky Feather (Right-click).
• *Effect:* Grants Levitation II for 10 seconds.
• *Cooldown:* 20 seconds.

_*Blood Sacrifice*_
• *Trigger:* Kill a Pig named "Vessel" near a Lectern with a Book & Quill.
• *Effect:* Permanently increases Max Health to 24 HP (+4 Hearts) until death.
• *Cooldown:* One-time ritual per spawn (60s Soul Sickness debuffs applied: Weakness, Darkness and Slowness).

_*Blood Mend*_
• *Trigger:* Use Enchanting Table with damaged item (Mainhand) + XP Bottle (Offhand).
• *Effect:* Repairs 200 durability to the item.
• *Cooldown:* Costs your Vigor (Reverses Blood Sacrifice) and causes Soul Sickness.

_*Summoner*_
• *Trigger:* Offhand Amethyst Shard and Book & Quill interaction with Lectern traps the soul of the mob trapped underneath you. 
• *Effect:* Use Summon item and make it fight for you for 2 minutes, or sell it to an ally team.
• *Cooldown:* 5-second channeling delay on summon.

*Custom Recipe:*
_*Sky Feather:* Feather + Experience Bottle_
_*Nether Wart:*_ Seeds, gold nugget and redstone
_*Experience Bottle:*_ Glass bottle (middle bottom), Lapis Lazuli (middle), and 7 Gold Nuggets

*Non-role Nerf:*
_*Fragile Anvils:* Non-Enchanters have a 10% chance to destroy anvils on touch!_
